About us

Welcome to the unvelling of Edusakha's new website! We are excited to showcase our latest project, which aims to create a deeper connection between students and their college community. Through attending events and communicating with alumni using chat, we hope to enhance the overall university experience for students.

Our team has worked tirelessly to create a website that is both user-friendly and Innovative. With live streaming and playback options, registration page for students, and event management for the university and its alumni, our website provides a comprehensive platform for students to engage with their college community.
